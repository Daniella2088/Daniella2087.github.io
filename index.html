WHO WE ARE

Summit Capital is an investment firm specializing in partnering with family and management-owned businesses. We offer flexible capital solutions and customized value creation programs aimed at accelerating growth for each business. Our mission is to establish authentic partnerships with leading businesses, taking a stewardship-driven approach to deliver meaningful outcomes for the business, its employees, and the communities it serves. Our core values center around stewardship, transparency, and respect for all stakeholders. Led by founder Rex Rokahr, Summit Capital draws from his background of working in a family business, which was started by his grandfather. These early experiences instilled in Rex the values of hard work, dedication, and integrity essential for serving customers, managing operations, and supporting employees. With our firsthand experience in family businesses and our history of investing in founder/family-owned enterprises, we possess a unique ability to comprehend and establish genuine partnerships with similar businesses
